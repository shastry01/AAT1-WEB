About Udupi

Udupi is a well-known coastal city in Karnataka that attracts visitors with its historic temples, calm beaches, and distinct food culture. The city offers a peaceful environment where tradition and nature coexist.

  • Ancient temples
  • Scenic coastline
  • Authentic cuisine
Ideal travel season: October to February
